Yoshitaka Sugita

Photographer / Data Scientist

Biography

Yoshitaka Sugita is a senior data scientist researching machine learning for predicting customer behavior and privacy-preserving technologies. He was born in Japan in 1986 and now lives in the United States. He received his Master of Engineering in Computer Science from Tokyo Institute of Technology.

His photography is inspired by his research activities. Through photography, he explores algorithms that intervene between the visual images and our brains.
